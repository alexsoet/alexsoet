Hello, this is my apartment, situated in De Pijp, Amsterdam. The apartment is situated in the lively De Pijp district and a mere 5 minute walk from Museum Quarter. Looking out of the window, you will have a view over the canal as boats pass by. Glance to your right to see the famous Rijksmuseum. After having spent a day in Amsterdam, there are many bars and restaurants in the neighbourhood for you to enjoy!
